Weekly Support Group

A judgment-free hour that's just for you

Every Wednesday evening, caregivers gather online to talk honestly about the journey — the hard parts, the wins, and everything in between. Certified facilitation, zero judgment, real connection.

Register once and you're on the list — reminders and the meeting link come to you.

Wellness Grants

A personalized stipend for your well-being

Caregivers spend so much on everyone else. Wellness Grants fund the things that restore you — a massage, counseling, a gym membership — and every award comes with its own care package.

Sanctuary members are eligible. Apply online; our team reviews each application and personalizes the award to your wellness plan.

Memory Socials — Drop-In Respite

A welcoming place for your loved one — a real break for you

Memory Socials are supervised gatherings for people living with dementia: familiar activities, gentle social connection, and no pressure to participate in any certain way. You can step away knowing your loved one is in trained, caring hands.

Memory Social Caregiver Events run alongside — while your loved one is engaged, you join other caregivers for wellness, connection, or education. Together in the same space, each of you cared for.
